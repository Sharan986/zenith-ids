1

Problem Definition
Not Industry-Ready
Theory-focused education with no real-world skills development.
Limited Opportunities
Industries restricted to Steel, Power, Mining, Automobile
 with low job diversity.
Broken Hiring System
Resumes don't reflect real skills. Clarity of Job Description.
Untapped Tourism Economy
Culture, handicrafts, and nature exist but lack student involvement and MSME pipeline.
Result: Talent leaves Jharkhand and economy stays underdeveloped

Students ≠ Industry ≠ Tourism
Insight: Jharkhand has talent + tourism + culture but no system connecting them to jobs.
